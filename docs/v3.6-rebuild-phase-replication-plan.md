# v3.6 Rebuild Phase Replication Plan

Status: completed and superseded as the active execution brief by `docs/v3.7-next-phase.md`.

This note records the next approved phase after the locked `v3.5` baseline.

The goal is not to change the app contract.

The goal is to rebuild the geometry/tile product family so the app reproduces the
accepted `v3.5` visual and interaction contract with cleaner, fully formalized
preprocessing and refreshed shipped artifacts.

## Rebuild Goal

Rebuild and reship, without behavioral drift:

1. `Current` runtime board product
2. `2026` runtime board product
3. `Current` split-ICB exact and runtime products
4. shared-edge/topology products
5. group/selection outline products
6. split internal-arc helper product
7. `Current` and `2026` land/sea masks
8. facility preprocessing outputs

## Red Lines

- Do not change sidebar structure, control semantics, or store contracts.
- Do not change Region ordering, colour assignment, border semantics, or highlight logic.
- Do not change split-ICB runtime behavior unless the rebuilt products exactly preserve it.
- Do not rebuild masks from anything except the final shipped runtime family.
- Do not leave `Current` and `2026` on different architectural standards.

## Required Output Contract

The rebuilt products must preserve:

- current map Region colour assignment
- current map border/arc behavior
- selected board highlight behavior
- selected dissolved Region highlight behavior
- split-ICB special-case behavior
- facility selection and coastal inward-treatment behavior
- existing sidebar/config wiring

## Accepted `Current` Split Contract To Preserve

For `E54000042` Hampshire and Isle of Wight:

- parent-wide facility-seeded assignment remains the accepted prototype basis
- Isle of Wight stays held to `London & South`
- `Downlands & Forest North` stays `South West`

Accepted spot checks:

- `Badger Farm and Oliver's Battery` -> `South West`
- `Bishop's Waltham` -> `London & South`
- `Downlands & Forest North` -> `South West`
- `Totland & Colwell` -> `London & South`

## Land / Sea Requirement

The rebuild phase must produce a fresh land/sea mask family from the rebuilt
final shipped runtime products so the accepted coast behavior is replicated from
one formalized source chain.

## Success Condition

The rebuild is complete only when:

- the app still reads and behaves like locked `v3.5`
- the rebuilt products are formalized and reproducible
- the shipped masks, outlines, arcs, fills, and facilities all come from the
  rebuilt family without ad hoc repair steps outside the documented pipeline

## Formalization Step Now In Place

The first `v3.6` execution slice is to make the rebuild pipeline fail-fast when
the shipped family drifts.

That validator is now expected at the end of the runtime-family build:

- `scripts/validate-runtime-geometry-family.mjs`

It checks the accepted shipped-family contract for:

- `Current` and `2026` board counts and hole-free output
- `Current` and `2026` topology-edge products
- scenario board/outline derivatives built from the runtime families
- `Current` split runtime counts and helper-arc counts
- accepted Hampshire split spot checks
- local land/sea patch extents
- split validity and neighbor-overlap limits
- facility containment inside assigned shipped `Current` and shipped `2026` boards
